The Rules

Everything you need to know.

1. How To Enter

  • Pick exactly one team from each of the eight pots.
  • Pick the player you think will be the tournament's top goalscorer.
  • Submit your entry via the form on the Enter page.
  • You'll receive bank transfer details by email.
  • Your entry is only confirmed once payment is received.
  • Entries close before the tournament's first match.

3. Scoring System

Points are cumulative — a team picks up points for every stage it passes through. Goals are only counted from 90-minute play (extra time and penalties do not score).

Worked example:

Say you picked Brazil, and Brazil:

  • Finish 1st in their group → 8 points
  • Win their Last 32 match → 4 points
  • Win their Last 16 match → 8 points
  • Win their Quarter Final → 10 points
  • Win their Semi Final → 15 points
  • Win the Final → 25 points
  • Score 12 goals across all their matches → 24 points (12 × 2)

Brazil's total contribution: 94 points. Add the points from your other 7 teams plus the Golden Boot bonus (40 points) if you guessed right.

4. Prizes

The total prize pot is the sum of all entry fees. It's split between the top three:

  • 1st place: 60% of the pot
  • 2nd place: 25% of the pot
  • 3rd place: 15% of the pot
Final percentages may be adjusted depending on total entries. Winners paid by bank transfer within 7 days of the final.

5. Tiebreakers

If two or more entrants finish on equal points, the tiebreaker is:

  1. Total goals scored by your eight teams
  2. Highest single-team score
  3. Date of entry submission (earlier wins)

6. Fair Play

  • One entry per person.
  • Same email cannot be used twice.
  • The organiser's decisions on scoring disputes are final.
  • If a team withdraws from the tournament before kick-off, affected entrants will be offered a re-pick from the same pot.

7. Questions?

Get in touch with the organiser. Contact details will be added before launch.
